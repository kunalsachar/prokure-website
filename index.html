Treatments Offered

Cold Laser Therapy

Cold laser therapy is a painless, non-invasive treatment that uses low-level lasers to reduce inflammation, relieve pain, and promote tissue healing. It's commonly used for joint pain, soft tissue injuries, and arthritis.

Ultrasound Therapy

Is a treatment that uses high-frequency sound waves to promote healing in soft tissues. It helps reduce pain,improves blood flow and speeds up recovery by stimulating deep tissues without any discomfort.

Pelvic floor Physiotherapy

Focuses on strengthening and relaxing the muscles of the pelvic floor to treat issues like incontinence, pelvic pain, and postpartum recovery. It helps improve core stability, bladder control, and overall pelvic health.

Shockwave Therapy

Shockwave therapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses high-energy sound waves to stimulate healing in injured tissues. It's commonly used for chronic pain, tendonitis and muscle injuries to promote blood flow, reduce pain, and speed up recovery. Exampleas are Achilies tendonitis, carpal tunnel syndrome, tennis elbow,etc

Vestibular and concussion treatment

Vestibular and concussion treatment in physiotherapy focuses on managing dizziness, balance issues, and symptoms following a concussion. It includes exercises to retrain the brain, improve stability, and reduce symptoms like headaches, nausea, and light sensitivity for a safe return to daily activities.

IMS (Intramuscular Stimulation) / Dry Needling

IMS (Intramuscular Stimulation) / Dry Needling is a technique where thin needles are inserted into tight or painful muscles to release tension, improve mobility, and reduce pain. It targets trigger points and is often used for chronic pain and muscle dysfunction.

Manual therapy & Graston Technique

Manual therapy is a hands-on physiotherapy technique used to mobilize joints, reduce pain, and improve movement. It includes techniques like joint manipulation and soft tissue massage. Graston Technique is a specialized form of manual therapy that uses stainless steel tools to gently scrape and massage the skin, breaking down scar tissue and fascial restrictions to enhance healing and mobility.
